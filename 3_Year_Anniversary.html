Us Now:

She is the Love of my Life
Where I Love Her with all my Might.
Every second I get to be with Her,
The more overwhelmed I am with joy,
Knowing we are truly together.
She is the lil cupcake,
That makes my life sweeter, But my heart ache.
She is a one of a kind, yet we both share a similar mind
She is the Monika to my MC,
the noodles to my Ramen.
And She is....You..
